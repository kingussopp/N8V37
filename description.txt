N8V37 is a platform that updates the traditional educational system into a free and internet based one.
.the platform will have courses, TDs, TPs, quizzes and eventual evalutions that will enable the students to verify their improvement anytime.
..the courses will be in PDF version with an option to add more formats if possible.
..the TDs will also be in PDF format mainly with a "focus" option where the student can choose to restrict his access to solutions of the exercices they didn't finish yet.
..The platform will have a text editor to execute the TP scripts with a suggested solution , they will also have links to better editors(vscode, intellijcode,eclipse,....) depending on the caracterics of every TP.
..the quizzes will have 3 phases :
...the answer : the student will have MCQ format with a theory oriented questions .
...the scoring : the student will have access to scores he got from every question and an eventual general score.
...the evalution : detailed answers for the MCQ so the student can either improve their answer or rectify it.
.the platform will have three roles : 
..the student : this is the default role and will enable the user to view the ressources in the platforman dinteract with them
..the teacher : this role will enable its user to add or remove sections in the platform and manage the ressourcesof the platform.
..the supervisor : its sole role will be to manage the teacher role and may fill the shoes of a teacher.
.it will be based on the Laravel framework.
